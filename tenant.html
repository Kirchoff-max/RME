How it works

are you a

Search And Select

Browse through hundreds of properties within any city of your choice. Save your favorites and
set up search alerts so you don't miss your dream place! Our virtual tours, and detailed home and
neighbourhood descriptions will help you make your decision. Don't forget to check the landlord
polices before you book!



Book And Reserve the property

When you make a reservation that property is blocked until the landlord responds to your request
(for up to 24 hours). Once the landlord accepts, your payment method will automatically be
charged. As an extra security setup, we will transfer this money to the landlord 48 hours after you
move in, unless you contact us with a problem.



Confirmation

As soon as the landlord accepts the booking, your payment method will automatically be charged.
We will put you in contact with the landlord via email, so you can arrange the move-in time, key
collection, and transfer any documentation that they requested in their polices.



Move in

And that's it; the home is yours! The landlord will now be your main point of contact, but will still
be here in case you have any issues. All you need to do is collect your keys and sign your contract.
The first payment - which you made at the beginning of the booking process - will be transferred
to the landlord 48 hours after you move in.
